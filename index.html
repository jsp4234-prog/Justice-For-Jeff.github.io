Case Narrative

On Nov. 19, 2021, I sustained a work-related spinal injury. By Feb. 7, 2022, USPS management signed a binding agreement (PS Form 2499) limiting my work to 9-hour shifts.

Starting Jan. 14, 2023, management allegedly ordered me to violate these limits. This forced work reportedly worsened my condition, causing medically necessary absences.

USPS then used those exact absences to justify my removal on Mar. 5, 2024.

The Cost

I have lost my career and health insurance while managing a chronic condition. This lawsuit is about protecting my earned pension and ensuring federal laws are respected.

Amidst this legal battle, I am also grieving the recent passing of my beloved wife, Toni. Balancing this complex litigation while processing such a profound loss has been the greatest challenge of my life.
